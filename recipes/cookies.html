Chocolate Chip Cookies

My tried and true chocolate chip cookie recipe. My favorite way to elevate this recipe further is to skip the chocolate chips, and instead grab a couple of high quality chocolate bars. I like to do a mix of both milk and dark chocolate.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups brown sugar
  • 1 stick cold butter, cubed
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 1/2 cups flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 heaping tsp baking soda
  • 8 oz chocolate

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F
  2. Combine brown sugar and butter using a hand mixer until there are no lumps of butter
  3. Add in eggs and mix until creamed
  4. Add vanilla, salt, and baking soda and Combine
  5. Carefully mix in the flour 1/2 cup at a time (be careful not to overmix)
  6. Fold in chocolate chunks
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes and allow to cool for at least 20 minutes
