mint limeade

A refreshing beverage that is completely customizable to your taste preference! It also pairs well with an added bottle of semi-sweet white wine for a minty and citrusy sangria.

ingredients

  • 4 cups water
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1 cup fresh lime juice
  • 1/3 cup mint leaves
  • Ice
  • Lime wedges and mint leaves for garnish

steps

  1. Dissolve sugar in 2 cups of water, warmed, and add remaining 2 cups water
  2. In a blender, combine water, lime juice, and mint leaves
  3. Blend until fully combined
  4. Optionally, strain mint leaves or keep for a stronger flavor
  5. Pour over ice and garnish with fresh lime wedges and mint leaves
